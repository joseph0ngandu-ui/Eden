# Risk Management

## Risk Ladder
- <$30: 20%
- $30–$50: 10%
- $50–$100: 10%
- $100–$500: 5%
- $500–$1000: 3%
- $1000+: 1%

## UltraSmall Caps
- Dollar risk caps per tier limit absolute loss

## Position Sizing
- ATR-based TP/SL define R
- Trail to BE at +0.8R

## Gating
- Confidence thresholds
- Session windows (UTC 06–12, 14–18, 20–23)
- ATR top-quantile filter
